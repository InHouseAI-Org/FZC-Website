TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE PE 504

Style PE 504 is manufactured from expanded PTFE with graphite, molecularly bonded to provide enhanced sealing performance. The unique combination delivers excellent chemical resistance, superior heat dissipation, and high resilience, resulting in extended service life and reliable operation in demanding industrial environments.

Its low-friction characteristics help minimize wear and erosion on shafts and sleeves while maintaining dimensional stability under varying operating conditions. Requiring minimal gland pressure, PE 504 performs efficiently at high speeds with low leakage rates. It is compatible with a wide range of industrial media, except strong oxidizers, molten alkali metals, and fluorine compounds.

The packing is ideally suited for pumps, valves, reactors, agitators, and other rotating equipment where dependable sealing and long-term reliability are required.
