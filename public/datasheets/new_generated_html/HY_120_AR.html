HY 120 AR

Engineered Ramie Fibre Packing for Marine and Water Handling Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 120 AR

Inmarco HY 120 AR is a premium-grade economical packing braided from extremely strong Ramie Fibre yarn and further impregnated with further reinforcement of strong aramid fibre enhance its chemical and abrasion resistance resulting in superior sealing performance.

The combination of natural Ramie fibres and aramid provide excellent abrasion resistance, reduced friction, and improved durability compared to conventional cotton fibre packings. Its dense construction allows optimum retention of lubricants and dispersants, resulting in superior sealing performance and extended service life.

HY 120 AR offers an economical alternative to synthetic packings and is particularly suitable for water handling mind particles e.g., industrial application containing sea water and marine industry where reliability and cost-effectiveness are essential.
