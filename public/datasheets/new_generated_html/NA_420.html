NA 420

Compressed Non-Asbestos Fiber Jointing Sheet

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

NA 420

NA 420 is a high-quality compressed non-asbestos fiber (CNAF) jointing sheet manufactured from a blend of mineral fibers, aramid fibers, and inorganic fillers bonded with a high-performance NBR binder. It is designed to provide reliable sealing performance in medium-stress applications involving oils, fuels, steam, water, gases, hydrocarbons, and a wide range of industrial chemicals.

The material offers excellent tensile strength, recovery, gas tightness, and resistance to oils and fuels. Its balanced combination of mechanical strength and sealing capability makes it suitable for general industrial gasket applications where dependable performance and durability are required.
