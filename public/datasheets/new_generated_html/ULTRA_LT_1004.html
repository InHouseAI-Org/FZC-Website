ULTRA LT 1004

Advanced Ultra-Low Friction PTFE-Based Fugitive Emission Combination Packing for Low-Temperature Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

ULTRA LT 1004

Inmarco ULTRA LT 1004 is an advanced ultra-low friction PTFE-based fugitive emission combination packing specifically engineered for low-temperature and cryogenic applications requiring exceptional sealing performance, minimal leakage, and long-term operational reliability. The packing features a unique construction consisting of intermediate rings braided from high-quality PTFE filament combined with sintered PTFE end rings, ensuring superior mechanical stability, chemical resistance, and dimensional integrity under extreme temperature conditions.

Designed to meet the stringent requirements of fugitive emission control, ULTRA LT 1004 delivers virtually zero friction characteristics (friction factor 0.1), outstanding low-temperature performance, and enhanced adaptability to thermal cycling. Its combination of braided PTFE intermediate rings and sintered end rings provides uniform sealing pressure distribution, minimal shaft wear, and extended service life in demanding cryogenic, LNG, and refrigeration applications.

ULTRA LT 1004 is tested and certified according to ISO 15848 and TA-Luft (2021) standards, ensuring compliance with international fugitive emission regulations. The packing is suitable for critical valves, pumps, and rotating equipment operating in low-temperature service conditions where conventional packings may fail due to thermal contraction, embrittlement, or loss of sealing integrity.
