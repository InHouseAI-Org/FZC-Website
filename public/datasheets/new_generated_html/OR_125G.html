OR 125G

Proprietary Graphite Dispersion for Superior Chemical Resistance

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

OR 125G

Inmarco Style OR 125G is a premium-grade packing manufactured from high-strength acrylic fiber. It is impregnated with a proprietary graphite-based dispersion, enhancing its chemical resistance, thermal conductivity, and sealing performance. The packing offers excellent mechanical integrity, durability, and operational reliability.

Its advanced composition ensures minimal shaft wear and superior lubrication while promoting efficient heat dissipation during operation. Designed for consistent performance, Style OR 125G is an economical and efficient solution for a wide range of industrial sealing applications.
