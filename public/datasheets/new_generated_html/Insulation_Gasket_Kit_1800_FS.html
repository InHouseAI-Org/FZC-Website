TECHNICAL DATASHEET

INSULATION GASKET KIT 1800 FS

Inmarco Insulation Gasket Kit 1800 FS is a high-integrity flange isolation and sealing system designed for critical applications where fire safety, fluid containment, and electrical insulation are essential. It is specifically engineered for offshore, petrochemical, refinery, and high-pressure industrial environments where prevention of leakage and galvanic corrosion is critical to operational safety.

The kit features a metal-reinforced epoxy composite carrier plate combined with a dual sealing system that ensures reliable performance under both normal operating conditions and fire-exposure scenarios. The design effectively isolates flange faces electrically while maintaining high-pressure sealing integrity.

The advanced construction prevents galvanic corrosion between dissimilar flange materials and minimizes the risk of secondary system damage caused by leakage, fire exposure, or electrical continuity failure.
