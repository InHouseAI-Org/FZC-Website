TECHNICAL DATASHEET

CG 102

INMARCO Style CG 102 is manufactured from premium expanded graphite yarn containing a minimum of 98% carbon content and reinforced with resilient Inconel wire. The packing is impregnated with a proprietary graphite-based dispersion and a corrosion inhibitor system that protects equipment against galvanic corrosion while enhancing sealing performance in demanding applications.

The flexible Inconel wire reinforcement provides excellent mechanical strength and pressure resistance, while the self-lubricating graphite structure assists stem adjustment during gland tightening. The advanced graphite-based lubricant minimizes leakage in both static and dynamic equipment, making Style CG 102 an ideal solution for valves, autoclaves, rotary kilns, and other high-temperature sealing applications.
