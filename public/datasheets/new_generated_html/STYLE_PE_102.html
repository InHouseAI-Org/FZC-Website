TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE PE 102

Style PE 102 is a premium inter-braided packing manufactured from expanded PTFE and graphite fibre. This advanced molecular blend combines the exceptional chemical resistance of PTFE with the thermal conductivity and lubricating properties of graphite, delivering superior sealing performance compared to conventional PTFE-graphite packings.

The packing utilizes high-performance K-fibre technology from WL Gore Inc., with graphite molecularly bonded during the polymerization process. This unique construction prevents graphite migration and disintegration, maintains continuous lubrication, and ensures long-term structural integrity. PE 102 is particularly suitable for pumps operating without cooling water, providing reliable sealing, reduced friction, and enhanced equipment protection.
