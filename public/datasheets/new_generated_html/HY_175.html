TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 175

HY 175 is a combination of duplex braided packing with inmalon yarn and expanded PTFE intimately bonded with special quality graphite yarn incorporating break-in-lubricant in a special process. This is a dense packing having high degree of resiliency and chemical properties.HY 175 the presence of graphite enables the packing dissipate heat accurately leading to longer life of the packing. The break-in-lubricant ensures extra lubrication during the complete operational life.HY 175 is highly anti-frictional and hence able to safeguard shaft/sleeves from wear/erosion. This packing is having excellent resistance to almost all known chemicals and is compatible to wide range of industrial media and is only affected by strong oxidizers, molten alkali metal, fluorine compounds, concentrated fuming acids.HY 175 is suitable for dynamic as well as static application. As it is dimensionally stable it ensures leakage free operational life for a longer period.
