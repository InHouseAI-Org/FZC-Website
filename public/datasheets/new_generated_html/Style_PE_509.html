STYLE PE 509

Pure PTFE Packing for General Industrial Sealing Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE PE 509

Inmarco Style PE 509 is a high-performance braided compression packing manufactured from 100% pure PTFE, designed for general industrial sealing applications requiring excellent chemical resistance, thermal stability, and clean operational performance. The packing provides reliable sealing under a broad range of operating conditions while maintaining low maintenance requirements and extended service life.

Its chemically inert and non-toxic composition makes it suitable for applications involving sensitive process media where contamination-free sealing is essential. The square braided structure ensures dimensional stability, uniform sealing pressure, and consistent operational reliability in both static and dynamic applications.

Style PE 509 offers excellent resistance to aggressive chemicals, thermal stress, and environmental degradation while maintaining flexibility and sealing integrity over extended operating periods. Its low dust generation and skin-friendly properties support safe handling and cleaner working environments. The packing is environmentally safe, recyclable, and suitable for a wide variety of industrial process applications requiring dependable sealing performance and operational safety.
