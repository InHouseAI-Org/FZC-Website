SOFT IRON RING

Inmarco Soft Iron Ring is a precision-machined metallic sealing component designed for high-pressure valve systems and critical sealing applications where reliable metal-to-metal sealing performance is required. Manufactured from high-quality soft iron and optionally supplied with silver-plated sealing surfaces, the ring provides excellent surface conformity and dependable sealing under severe operating conditions.

The pressure-activated sealing design utilizes internal system pressure to increase sealing efficiency as operating pressure rises. This self-tightening mechanism enables superior leak-tight performance in high-pressure valve bonnets, steam systems, pressure vessels, and other critical industrial applications.

Designed for demanding thermal and pressure cycling environments, Soft Iron Rings provide excellent dimensional stability, repeatable sealing performance, and long service life. Standard and custom dimensions are available to suit a wide range of valve and equipment designs.
