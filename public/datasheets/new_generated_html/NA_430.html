NA 430

Premium Non-Asbestos Fiber Jointing Sheet for High-Performance Sealing

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

NA 430

NA 430 is a premium compressed non-asbestos fiber jointing sheet reinforced with SS304 stainless steel wire mesh for enhanced mechanical strength and dimensional stability. Manufactured using a high-performance calendaring process, it consists of high-tenacity aramid fibers blended with organic fibers and bonded with NBR.

The stainless steel wire mesh reinforcement improves pressure resistance, durability, and gasket integrity under demanding operating conditions. NA 430 is designed for medium- to high-stress sealing applications involving oils, fuels, steam, water, hydrocarbons, and a wide range of industrial chemicals.
