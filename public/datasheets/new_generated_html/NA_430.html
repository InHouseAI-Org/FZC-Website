Performance Benefits

Premium Non-Asbestos Fiber Jointing Sheet for High-Performance Sealing

● NA 430 is a premium compressed non-asbestos fiber jointing sheet reinforced with SS304 stainless steel wire mesh for enhanced mechanical strength and dimensional stability. Manufactured using a high-performance calendaring process, it consists of high-tenacity aramid fibers blended with organic fibers and bonded with NBR.
● The stainless steel wire mesh reinforcement improves pressure resistance, durability, and gasket integrity under demanding operating conditions. NA 430 is designed for medium- to high-stress sealing applications involving oils, fuels, steam, water, hydrocarbons, and a wide range of industrial chemicals.

Applications

● Pumps, compressors, and pipe flange connections.
● Oil and fuel processing systems.
● Steam and hot water applications.
● Petrochemical and chemical processing equipment.
● Hydrocarbon handling systems.
● Cooling water circuits and utility services.
● Industrial machinery requiring high-performance gasket materials.
● Applications involving diluted acids, alkalis, lubricants, and industrial fluids.

Performance Benefits

● SS304 wire mesh reinforcement provides enhanced mechanical strength and blowout resistance.
● High tensile strength ensures durability under pressure and thermal cycling.
● Excellent recovery characteristics maintain gasket integrity during fluctuating operating conditions.
● Low stress relaxation improves long-term sealing reliability.
● Superior resistance to oils, fuels, hydrocarbons, steam, and cooling liquids.
● Excellent gas tightness minimizes leakage and enhances process safety.
● Suitable for operating pressures up to 120 bar.
● Performs reliably at temperatures up to 300°C, with peak resistance up to 400°C.
● Ideal for medium- and high-stress industrial sealing applications.

Service Range

● NA 430 is a premium metallic-grade gasket sheet suitable for oils, fuels, lubricants, alcohols, hydrocarbons, steam, water, cooling liquids, and most diluted acids and alkalis under medium-stress operating conditions.
● For applications falling outside the recommended pressure-temperature operating range, please consult the INMARCO Technical Department.

Important Note

● Gaskets should not be subjected to maximum temperature and maximum pressure simultaneously.
