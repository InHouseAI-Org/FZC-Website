TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE PE 104C

Style PE 104C is a high-performance gland packing specifically engineered for control valves operating under extreme industrial conditions. Designed to provide exceptional resilience, mechanical strength, and sealing reliability, it is ideally suited for high-pressure and high-temperature applications where long-term sealing performance is critical.

The packing features a reinforced carbon yarn core treated with advanced fluoropolymer dispersion and corrosion inhibitors, surrounded by a pure PTFE yarn jacket lubricated with PTFE suspensoid. This sophisticated construction provides superior chemical resistance, excellent thermal stability, low friction operation, and outstanding leakage control in frequently operated control valves.

Its ability to conform to stuffing box irregularities, combined with excellent recovery characteristics and heat dissipation properties, ensures reliable sealing performance and extended service life in demanding industrial environments.
