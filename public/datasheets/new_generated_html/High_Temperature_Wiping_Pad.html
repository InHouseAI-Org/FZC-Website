High Temperature Wiping Pad

INMARCO High Temperature Wiping Pad is manufactured from a specially engineered blend of Carbon Fiber, Volcanic Rock Fiber, and Alumino Silicate Fiber. Braided using an advanced manufacturing process, the pad provides exceptional density, durability, and dimensional stability under demanding operating conditions.

The wiping pad is impregnated with a specially formulated PTFE Suspensoid, ensuring efficient wiping action and consistent zinc coating thickness during galvanizing operations. Its unique fiber composition offers outstanding resistance to heat, abrasion, and chemical attack, making it particularly suitable for continuous wire processing applications.

Designed for wire drawing and galvanizing industries, the High Temperature Wiping Pad delivers a service life up to 3–4 times longer than conventional co-polymer yarn pads, resulting in reduced downtime and lower maintenance costs.
