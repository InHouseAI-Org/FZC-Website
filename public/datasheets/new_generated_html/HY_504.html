HY 504

Enhanced Duplex Braided Packing with Superior Thermal Dissipation

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 504

Inmarco 504 is manufactured from premium-grade expanded PTFE and graphite yarns molecularly bonded to provide superior sealing performance. The packing offers excellent chemical resistance, exceptional heat dissipation, and high resiliency, ensuring long operational life in demanding industrial applications.

Its anti-friction characteristics help protect shafts and sleeves from wear and erosion while maintaining dimensional stability under varying operating conditions. The packing requires minimal gland loading to achieve an effective seal and performs reliably at high peripheral speeds with extremely low leakage rates.

504 is suitable for pumps, valves, agitators, reactors, and other rotating equipment handling a wide range of industrial media. The packing is tested and qualified as per EN 16752 (Centrifugal Pumps Test Procedure for Seal Packing), Tightness Class T1.
