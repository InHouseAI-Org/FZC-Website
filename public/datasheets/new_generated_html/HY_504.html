TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 504

HY 504is an improved version of duplex braided packing manufactured from Expanded PTFE intimately entrapped with high quality graphite reinforced with high structural carbon fibers. The PTFE graphite yarn is made in single molecular structure. This is a dense packing having high degree of resiliency and chemical properties. Presence of graphite & carbon enables the packing dissipate heat accurately leading to longer life of the packing.HY 504is highly anti-frictional and hence able to safeguard shaft/sleeves from wear/erosion. This packing is having excellent resistance to almost all known chemicals and is compatible to wide range of industrial media and is only affected by strong oxidizers, molten alkali metal and fluorine compounds.HY 504is dimensionally stable and requires minimum gland loading to effect perfect seal. This is also compatible to very high peripheral speed with extremely low leakage rate. This packing is an excellent resistant to corrosion and abrasion.
