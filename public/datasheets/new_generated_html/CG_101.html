TECHNICAL DATASHEET

CG 101

CG 101 is a high-performance gland packing manufactured from premium carbon filament yarn and impregnated with a thermally cured graphite-based In-Therm dispersion. This proprietary treatment acts as a blocking agent, significantly reducing stem friction while enhancing sealing efficiency against both gaseous and liquid media.

The packing incorporates a passive corrosion inhibitor that protects equipment against galvanic corrosion, extending component life and maintaining sealing integrity. Its excellent heat dissipation characteristics ensure cooler shaft operation, while adequate lubrication provides exceptionally low-friction performance without sacrificing strength or flexibility.

Designed for demanding industrial environments, CG 101 delivers reliable sealing performance across a wide range of temperatures, pressures, and chemical media.
