TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 105T

INMARCO HY 105T is a high-performance gland packing manufactured using Aldehyde Yarn corners and PTFE Yarn faces, reinforced with a resilient PTFE cord core. Its dense braided construction provides excellent mechanical strength, dimensional stability, and sealing reliability under extreme operating conditions.

The packing is impregnated with advanced fluoropolymer and inert lubricating compounds that reduce friction and minimize wear during operation. HY 105T offers outstanding resistance to aggressive chemical media including ammonia, urea, carbamate, detergents, pigments, and synthetic materials.

Designed for demanding industrial applications, HY 105T delivers reliable sealing performance in fertilizer, chemical, paint, and process industries where high pressure, abrasive conditions, and chemical exposure are common.
