INMATEX™ 1400

Expanded PTFE Sheet for High-Integrity Flange Sealing

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

INMATEX™ 1400

INMARCO INMATEX™ TYPE 1400 is a premium expanded PTFE sheet manufactured from 100% virgin Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) using an advanced expansion process. The material is soft, flexible, and highly resilient, allowing excellent compressibility and conformance to flange sealing surfaces.

The sheet exhibits outstanding resistance to creep, hot flow, and cold flow while maintaining excellent sealing performance under low bolt loads. Its unique expanded PTFE structure provides superior permeation resistance, emission control, and long-term sealing reliability across a wide range of chemical and industrial applications.

INMATEX™ 1400 withstands compressive loads exceeding 1650 bar, making it suitable for demanding sealing applications involving fragile and non-metallic flanges.
