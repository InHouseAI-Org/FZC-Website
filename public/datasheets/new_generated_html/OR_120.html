OR 120

Economical Ramie Fibre Packing with PTFE Dispersion for Enhanced Chemical Resistance

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

OR 120

Inmarco Style OR 120 is a premium-grade economical packing braided from extremely strong Ramie fibre and impregnated with proprietary PTFE dispersion to enhance chemical resistance and sealing performance. The packing combines the natural strength of Ramie fibre with the lubricating and sealing properties of PTFE, making it a reliable and cost-effective solution for a wide range of industrial applications.

Its dense construction allows optimum retention of the PTFE dispersion, resulting in improved sealing efficiency, reduced leakage, and extended service life. OR 120 provides excellent abrasion resistance and mechanical durability while maintaining low friction during operation.
