TECHNICAL DATASHEET

CG 100

100FX is an expanded flexible pure graphite fibre (purity 99% minimum) braided packing reinforced with non-metallic filament. The packing is extrusion resistant and capable of withstanding higher pressure both in dynamic and static conditions.

CG 100 packing is impregnated with proprietary hi-therm dispersion to fill the leak paths between the braids. Under gland pressure it becomes a homogeneous mass. This advanced construction ensures reliable sealing performance with excellent resistance to high temperatures and pressures.

Key Features & Advantages

● Self generating lubrication accommodates and aids self adjustment on stem upon tightening of gland
● Most effective sealing performance with no scoring of sleeve, plunger or stem
● Hi-therm lubrication developed by in-house R&D prevents body and shaft leakage on static or dynamic equipment
● Suits for high mechanical and cylindrical load applications
● Excellent sealing during long duration operations
● Extrusion resistant and capable of withstanding higher pressure

Applications & Service Media

● Pumps, Valves, Dryers, Reactors
● Superheated & Saturated steam
● Gases, Petrochemicals, Hydrocarbon
● Thermic fluid, Hot oil
● Acids & Alkalis, Solvents
● Organic chemicals, Emissive fluids

Style CG 100

● Style CG 100 is a high-performance braided packing manufactured from 99% pure expanded graphite fiber reinforced with non-metallic filaments. Designed for demanding industrial sealing applications, it offers outstanding resistance to extrusion, high temperatures, aggressive chemicals, and high-pressure operating conditions in both dynamic and static services.
● The packing is impregnated with proprietary HiTherm dispersion, with PTFE dispersion available as an option, creating a dense and uniform sealing structure that effectively blocks leakage paths. Its self-lubricating properties allow automatic adjustment under gland pressure, reducing friction, minimizing wear, and extending equipment life.
● Developed through advanced in-house research and engineering, Style CG 100 provides exceptional sealing reliability, superior thermal stability, and long-term performance under severe mechanical loads and thermal cycling conditions.
