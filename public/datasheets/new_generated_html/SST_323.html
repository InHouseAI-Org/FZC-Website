Flexible Pure Graphite Tanged Gasket Sheet

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

SST 323

Inmarco SST 323 Flexible Pure Graphite Tanged Gasket Sheet is manufactured from perforated stainless steel tanged reinforcement mechanically bonded between two layers of high-purity flexible graphite sheet without the use of adhesives or bonding agents.

The combination of flexible graphite and stainless steel tanged reinforcement provides excellent mechanical strength, compressibility, recovery, and dimensional stability under severe operating conditions. With minimum graphite purity 99%, SST 323 delivers reliable sealing performance in high-temperature, high-pressure, cryogenic, and chemically aggressive environments.

The gasket sheet is highly resilient and effectively conforms to flange irregularities, ensuring dependable sealing performance even under thermal cycling and pressure fluctuations. SST 323 is suitable for metallic, glass-lined, enamel, and irregular flange surfaces where operational reliability and reduced maintenance are critical.
