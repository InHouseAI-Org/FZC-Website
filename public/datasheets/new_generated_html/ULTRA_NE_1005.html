Advanced Ultra-Low Emission All-Braided Packing for High-Performance Industrial Sealing Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

ULTRA NE 1005

Inmarco ULTRA NE 1005 is an advanced ultra-low emission all-braided compression packing specifically engineered for high-performance industrial sealing applications requiring exceptional fugitive emission control, superior chemical resistance, and long-term operational reliability. Manufactured from premium expanded flexible graphite with integrated Inconel wire mesh reinforcement and advanced lubrication technology, ULTRA NE 1005 delivers unmatched sealing performance under extreme pressure, temperature, and chemical exposure conditions.

The packing's sophisticated construction combines high-purity expanded graphite fibers with corrosion-resistant Inconel wire mesh reinforcement, providing outstanding mechanical strength, dimensional stability, and adaptability to shaft movement and thermal cycling. Its advanced lubrication system ensures smooth operation, minimal friction, reduced shaft wear, and extended service life across a wide range of dynamic and static sealing applications.

ULTRA NE 1005 is tested and certified as per API 622 (Latest Edition) and ISO 15848 with CC1 endurance class and tightness class AH, ensuring compliance with the most stringent international fugitive emission standards. The packing is fire-safe compliant and suitable for critical service applications in petrochemical, refinery, power generation, chemical processing, and other demanding industrial environments where zero-emission performance and operational safety are paramount.
