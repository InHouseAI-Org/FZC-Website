TECHNICAL DATASHEET

GM 300Z

GM 300Zis a high-efficiency, ultra-low oxidation, graphite-molded rings for valve stem and seat sealing. Each ring is made from flexible graphite foil with high purity (≥99.85%) and low detrimental material content. These rings have oxidation inhibitors and advanced corrosion-resistant properties, meeting stringent nuclear industry specifications. They are precisely sized, density-controlled, and free from binders, elastomers, or any fillers.

Key Features

● Ultra-High PurityGraphite purity ≥99.85% with ultra-low sulfur content (typical batch specific values below 50 ppm)
● Nuclear Grade SpecificationDesigned for demanding nuclear sector valve applications where high purity and low sulfur are essential
● Built-in Oxidation InhibitorAdvanced oxidation-resistant properties for extended service life

Performance Advantages

● Free from ContaminantsNo binders, elastomers, or fillers - precisely sized and density-controlled
● Excellent Thermal ConductivityAxial: 400W/mK; Radial: 6W/mK (at density 1.4g/cm³)
● Low Friction CoefficientCoefficient of friction to steel: 0.05 for smooth operation
