GM 300Z

High-Purity Graphite Molded Rings with Exceptional Oxidation Resistance

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

GM 300Z

Inmarco GM 300Z is a high-efficiency, ultra-low oxidation graphite molded ring designed for valve stem and seat sealing applications. Each ring is manufactured from flexible graphite foil with ultra-high purity (≥99.85%) and extremely low detrimental material content.

The rings incorporate advanced oxidation inhibitors and corrosion-resistant properties, meeting stringent requirements for nuclear and high-temperature service applications. They are precisely sized, density-controlled, and manufactured without binders, elastomers, or fillers, ensuring superior sealing reliability and long service life.

GM 300Z is specifically developed for critical valve applications handling high-temperature steam, hydrocarbons, heat transfer fluids, and other demanding process media where graphite purity and oxidation resistance are essential.
