High-Performance Duplex Braided Packing for Demanding Industrial Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE PE 505

Inmarco Style PE 505 is a high-performance duplex braided compression packing manufactured from a specially engineered combination of Expanded PTFE, Graphite, and MoS₂ bonded at the molecular level. The advanced material structure provides excellent chemical resistance, superior heat dissipation, low friction characteristics, and high dimensional stability under dynamic operating conditions.

Designed for demanding industrial sealing applications, Style PE 505 delivers reliable sealing performance with reduced shaft wear, low leakage rates, and extended service life. Its anti-frictional composition minimizes equipment wear while supporting smooth operation in high-speed rotating and reciprocating equipment.

The dense and resilient braided construction ensures consistent sealing integrity with minimal gland adjustment requirements, making it suitable for pumps, valves, agitators, mixers, reactors, and other critical industrial equipment operating under severe service conditions. Style PE 505 is compatible with a broad range of industrial media excluding strong oxidizers, molten alkali metals, and fluorine compounds.
