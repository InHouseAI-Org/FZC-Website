PA 499

PA 499 is an advanced synthetic packing tough, soft, flexible with enhanced sliding feature, impregnated with fluoropolymer dispersion. This new generation packing addresses the limitations of earlier synthetic packings, making it suitable for challenging applications combined with abrasion and chemical media.

By combining the durability of aramid with the low friction characteristics of material like carbon fiber, it stands out as an excellent choice for diverse applications. This innovative packing material offers flexibility, wear resistance, and efficient performance, while preventing abrasion and shaft damage, and is easy to install with minimal adjustments.

Style PA 499

● Style PA 499 is an advanced synthetic packing engineered to provide exceptional mechanical strength, flexibility, and sealing reliability in demanding industrial applications. Manufactured from a unique blend of high-performance novoloid fibres and impregnated with a proprietary fluoropolymer dispersion, it combines excellent abrasion resistance with superior sliding characteristics. It is tough, soft, flexible with enhanced sliding feature, delivers reliable sealing performance across a wide range of operating conditions.
● This next-generation packing addresses the limitations of conventional synthetic packings by offering outstanding performance in applications involving abrasive media, chemical exposure, and dynamic operating conditions. By combining the durability of aramid-based materials with the low-friction characteristics associated with advanced fibre technologies, Style PA 499 delivers extended equipment life, reduced maintenance, and enhanced operational efficiency.
● Its flexible construction allows easy installation and excellent conformity to stuffing box configurations while minimizing gland adjustment requirements and shaft wear.
