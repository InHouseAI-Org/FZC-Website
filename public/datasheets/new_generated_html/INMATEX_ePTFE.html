INMATEX ePTFE

INMATEX Expanded PTFE (ePTFE) Sheet / Gasketwith exceptional malleability for extreme operating conditions.With exceptionally uniform density and highly fibrillated micro structure (no unexpanded nodes), INMATEX multi-directional expanded PTFE (ePTFE) sheet/gasket has excellent resistance to pressure, creep relaxation, cold flow. It is made of 100% virgin PTFE. It is extremely soft but tough and highly compressible. It is non-staining and non-contaminating and one of the highest resistance to aggressive media, best in-class reliability as to seal flange joints and piping equipments.
