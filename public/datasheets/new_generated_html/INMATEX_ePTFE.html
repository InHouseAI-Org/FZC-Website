Multi-Directional Expanded PTFE Gasket for Superior Chemical Resistance and Low Seating Stress

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

INMATEX ePTFE

Inmarco INMATEX Expanded PTFE (ePTFE) Sheet / Gasket is manufactured from 100% virgin multi-directional expanded PTFE featuring a highly fibrillated microstructure with exceptionally uniform density and no unexpanded nodes.

The unique multi-directional structure provides outstanding resistance to creep relaxation, cold flow, and pressure deformation while maintaining exceptional flexibility and compressibility. INMATEX offers reliable sealing performance under low bolt loads and conforms easily to irregular flange surfaces, ensuring leak-free operation across a wide range of industrial applications.

The material is non-staining, non-contaminating, chemically inert, and highly resistant to aggressive media, making it ideal for sealing critical flange joints, process equipment, and piping systems operating under severe chemical and thermal conditions.
