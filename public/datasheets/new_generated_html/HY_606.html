HY 606

High-Performance Aldehyde Fibre Packing for Severe Industrial Conditions

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 606

Inmarco HY 606 is a high-performance compression packing manufactured from abrasion-resistant Aldehyde Fibre Yarn. The packing is impregnated with PTFE-based Inflon dispersion and treated with special break-in and run-in lubricants that significantly reduce friction while protecting shafts and sleeves from wear and erosion.

A resilient Red Inmalon yarn core provides additional structural strength, flexibility, and dimensional stability under demanding operating conditions. Its tough construction resists deformation and maintains sealing integrity even during prolonged service in abrasive and corrosive environments.

HY 606 is designed for use in pumps, valves, mixers, reactors, and other industrial equipment requiring reliable sealing performance under severe mechanical and chemical conditions.
