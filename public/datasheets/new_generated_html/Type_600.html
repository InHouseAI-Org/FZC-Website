TECHNICAL DATASHEET

TYPE 600

Inmarco TYPE 600 is a premium-grade 100% Virgin PTFE material manufactured for demanding industrial sealing, insulation, chemical handling, and engineering applications requiring exceptional chemical resistance, thermal stability, and low-friction performance.

Available in multiple forms including skived sheets, moulded sheets, rods, tubes, rings, and custom-machined components, TYPE 600 offers excellent inertness against most industrial chemicals and aggressive media.

PTFE is inherently non-flammable, chemically inert, and highly stable across a wide operating temperature range. Its extremely low coefficient of friction, excellent release characteristics, and outstanding electrical insulation properties make it ideal for critical applications in chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, food processing, electrical systems, oil & gas, and general industrial environments. TYPE 600 can be easily machined, fabricated, or moulded into customized sealing and engineering components while maintaining excellent dimensional stability and long-term operational reliability.
