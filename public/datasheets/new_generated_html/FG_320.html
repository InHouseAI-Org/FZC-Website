TECHNICAL DATASHEET

FG 320

INMARCO TYPE FG 320is manufactured from exfoliated graphite. During the initial process of exfoliation of graphite, acid treatment is done but 100% of acids are washed out by the DM water. After this, the whole process of manufacturing flexible graphite gasket sheet is mechanical, without incorporation of any additives, oils or bonding material.TYPE FG 320 flexible pure graphite sheet is having carbon content 99% to 99.9%. These are extremely suitable for high temperature and high pressure application. These gaskets are highly resilient and as such easily adopts to irregularities of flange or surface to make perfect seal. Inmarco flexible pure graphite gaskets are suitable for metallic, glass, enamel flanges and are designed for trouble free change over which ultimately reduces maintenance cost. They are dimensionally stable under extreme pressure surge. These are also suitable for cryogenic services.

Performance Benefits

Flexible Pure Graphite Sheet for High-Temperature and High-Pressure Sealing

● FG 320 is manufactured from exfoliated graphite. During the initial exfoliation process, graphite undergoes acid treatment; however, all residual acids are thoroughly removed using demineralized (DM) water. The subsequent manufacturing process is entirely mechanical and does not involve the addition of binders, fillers, oils, or other additives.
● The sheet consists of 99% pure carbon graphite, providing exceptional sealing performance in demanding high-temperature and high-pressure applications. Its highly resilient structure enables it to conform to flange irregularities and surface imperfections, ensuring reliable sealing performance.
● FG 320 is suitable for use with metallic, glass, and enamel flanges and is designed to facilitate easy maintenance and gasket replacement. The material remains dimensionally stable under pressure fluctuations and is also suitable for cryogenic services.
