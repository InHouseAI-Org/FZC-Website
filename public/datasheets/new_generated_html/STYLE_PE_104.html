Versatile Packing for Dynamic and Static Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE PE 104

Style PE 104 is a high-performance gland packing manufactured from premium virgin PTFE fibre yarn, interlock braided and impregnated with proprietary PTFE dispersion. Its chemically inert and non-toxic composition ensures maximum media purity while delivering reliable sealing performance across a broad range of industrial applications.

The interlock braided construction provides exceptional dimensional stability, resistance to disintegration, and superior mechanical integrity under demanding operating conditions. Designed for both dynamic and static sealing applications, Style PE 104 offers low friction operation, excellent chemical resistance, and extended service life with minimal maintenance requirements.

Its advanced PTFE formulation minimizes wear on shafts and sleeves while maintaining leak-free performance under varying pressures and temperatures, making it an ideal choice for pumps, valves, mixers, agitators, and process equipment.
