TECHNICAL DATASHEET

750 SS Welding Blanket

750 SS Welding Blanketis a heavy-duty fabric woven from high SiO₂ content yarn (≥96%) and treated with a specially formulated acid process.

This silica cloth is suitable for continuous service at 1100°C (2000°F) and can withstand short-term exposure up to 1400°C (2550°F).The fabric is available with optional Polyurethane or Vermiculite coatings, which enhance abrasion resistance and tensile strength.

It is ideal for heat preservation applications, firefighting equipment, fire curtains, welding blankets, fireproof garments, and high-temperature filtration systems. Style 750 SS is also widely used for manufacturing preformed fire and welding blankets with fiberglass tape edging and brass eyelets.

Key Features

●Manufactured from high SiO₂ content yarn (≥96%) for superior thermal performance.
●Suitable for continuous operation at temperatures up to 1100°C and short-term exposure up to 1400°C.
●Available with Polyurethane or Vermiculite coatings for enhanced abrasion resistance and durability.
●Satin weave construction provides excellent flexibility and mechanical strength.
●Offers outstanding resistance to thermal shock and extreme heat exposure.
●Suitable for use in high-temperature filtration and molten metal processing applications.

Applications

●Welding blankets and welding curtains.
●Fire curtains and fireproof safety equipment.
●Fireproof garments and thermal protection systems.
●Heat preservation and insulation applications.
●High-temperature dust collection and filtration systems.
●Molten metal filtration and industrial heat shielding applications.
