NA 432

Advanced Non-Asbestos Fibre Sheet with Superior Chemical Resistance

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

NA 432

NA 432 is a compressed non-asbestos fibre jointing sheet designed for general-purpose industrial sealing applications. It is manufactured from organic fibres bonded with high-quality NBR, providing reliable sealing performance, good compressibility, and excellent resistance to oils, fuels, water, and steam.

Complying with BS 7531 Grade Z, NA 432 offers a balanced combination of strength, flexibility, and sealing capability, making it suitable for medium-stress applications across a wide range of industrial equipment and piping systems.
