TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE CG 503

Inmarco Style CG 503 is a high-performance packing manufactured from expanded graphite and carbon fiber yarn, specifically developed for high-pressure and high-temperature valve applications. The packing core is reinforced with Inconel wire and protected by a fine wire mesh jacket, while the outer layer consists of low-friction carbon yarn. This advanced construction provides excellent sealing reliability in control valves, soot blowers, and other critical industrial services.

The packing is enhanced with an inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor and a proprietary graphite-based dispersion that helps prevent galvanic corrosion, improves heat dissipation, and reduces loss on ignition. Style CG 503 readily adapts to worn or pitted sealing surfaces, maintaining effective sealing performance without causing damage to equipment surfaces. Its robust construction ensures the packing remains intact during cutting, installation, and removal, reducing maintenance time and improving operational efficiency.
