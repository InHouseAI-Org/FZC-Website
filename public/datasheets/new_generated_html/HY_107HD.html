HY 107HD

High-Density Aramid & PTFE Packing for Fertilizer Plant Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 107HD

Inmarco HY 107HD is a high-density combination packing manufactured using Aramid Fibre Yarn at the corners and PTFE Graphite Yarn at the sealing faces, reinforced with a strong Aramid core. The packing is performed under heavy load according to plunger dimensions to ensure excusive shock and extrusion in challenging application. The packing is impregnated with an anti-friction fluoropolymer dispersion and a special break-in lubricant to provide excellent chemical resistance, dimensional stability, and thermal control.

The graphite-faced construction promotes efficient heat dissipation, while the Aramid reinforced corners provide exceptional structural strength and resistance to deformation under high pressure and dynamic loading conditions. Its dense braided construction delivers superior sealability and long-term sealing reliability in aggressive chemical services.

Specifically developed for critical fertilizer plant operations, HY 107HD performs exceptionally well in carbamate and urea pump applications where chemical resistance, mechanical strength, and operational reliability are essential.
