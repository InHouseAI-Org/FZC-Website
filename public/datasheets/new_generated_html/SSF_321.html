SSF 321

Stainless Steel Reinforced Flexible Graphite Sheet for High-Performance Industrial Sealing

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

SSF 321

Inmarco SSF 321 is a high-performance flexible graphite sheet manufactured by laminating a plain stainless steel reinforcement insert between layers of high-purity expanded graphite. The composite construction combines the superior sealing characteristics of flexible graphite with the enhanced mechanical strength and dimensional stability provided by stainless steel reinforcement.

The material is specifically engineered for high-temperature, high-pressure, and critical industrial sealing applications where long-term gasket integrity and operational reliability are essential. The reinforced graphite structure delivers excellent compressibility, recovery, blowout resistance, and sealing performance under severe thermal cycling and pressure fluctuation conditions.

SSF 321 is suitable for metallic, and enamel flange systems and is designed to provide dependable sealing performance in refinery, petrochemical, power generation, chemical processing, and cryogenic service environments. The material maintains excellent chemical resistance, low creep characteristics, and stable sealing performance across a broad operating range while reducing maintenance frequency and operational downtime.
