CORRUGATED METAL GASKET

Inmarco Corrugated Metal Gaskets are versatile semi-metallic sealing elements manufactured from precision-formed thin metallic sheets with concentric corrugations. The corrugated metal core is typically faced with soft sealing materials such as flexible graphite, PTFE, mica, ceramic fibre, or non-asbestos materials to enhance sealing performance and flange conformity.

The corrugated construction provides excellent resilience, recovery, and mechanical stability while requiring relatively low seating stress. These gaskets effectively accommodate flange irregularities, thermal expansion, vibration, and pressure fluctuations, making them suitable for demanding industrial applications.

Corrugated Metal Gaskets are widely used in chemical processing, petrochemical plants, refineries, power generation facilities, and oil & gas operations where reliable sealing under fluctuating operating conditions is essential.
