HY 107

Multi-Yarn Aramid & PTFE Packing for High-Performance Sealing

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 107

Inmarco HY 107 is a unique multi-yarn packing specifically designed for harsh industrial sealing applications. The packing combines Aramid filament yarn at the corners with graphite-lubricated PTFE yarn on the sealing faces and an Aramid filament core. The packing is performed according to plunger dimensions to ensure excusive shock and extrusion in challenging application. This engineered construction delivers the low friction characteristics of PTFE and graphite while providing superior abrasion resistance through high-strength Aramid reinforcement.

The dense braided structure ensures exceptional sealability by minimizing gaps and voids within the packing. When compressed, the packing forms a tight and reliable sealing barrier that effectively prevents fluid leakage even under demanding operating conditions.

HY 107 is designed to withstand high-pressure service while maintaining excellent dimensional stability and sealing integrity. Its outstanding resistance to abrasion, chemicals, Ammonia, and Carbamate solutions makes it suitable for a wide range of industrial applications where reliability, durability, and long service life are critical.
