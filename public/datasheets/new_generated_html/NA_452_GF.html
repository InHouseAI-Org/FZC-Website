NA 452 GF

Glass Fibre Reinforced Non-Asbestos Sheet for Enhanced Strength

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

NA 452 GF

NA 452 GF is a premium compressed non-asbestos Fibre jointing sheet manufactured from aramid Fibre, glass Fibre, and NBR binder. The incorporation of glass Fibre reinforcement significantly improves mechanical strength, dimensional stability, temperature resistance, and sealing reliability under high-stress operating conditions.

Manufactured in compliance with BS 7531 Grade X, NA 452 GF is designed for demanding industrial sealing applications involving oils, fuels, hydrocarbons, steam, water, cooling liquids, diluted acids, alkalis, and various industrial process media.
