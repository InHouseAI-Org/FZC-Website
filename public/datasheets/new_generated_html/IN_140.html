Fiberglass Braided Packing

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

IN 140

Inmarco Style IN 140 is a dry non-asbestos cover-on-cover braided packing manufactured from inorganic fibrous material based on refractory oxides. The packing offers excellent resistance to high temperatures and provides outstanding thermal and electrical insulation properties.

IN 140 is designed for static sealing applications operating under severe thermal conditions. The packing combines excellent thermal stability, chemical resistance, and mechanical resilience, making it suitable for a wide range of industrial sealing and insulation applications.

The product is fire retardant and certified as per IS 11871 method A and B (vertical and inclined flammability and UL 94 flammability). It is resistant to all types of gases, including flue gases, and exhibits excellent moisture resistance by repelling water rather than absorbing it. The packing is non-toxic, non-hazardous, and environmentally friendly.
