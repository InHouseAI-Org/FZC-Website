TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 105HD

Inmarco HY 105HD is a dense braided gland packing manufactured with Aramid Yarn corners and PTFE Yarn faces, reinforced by a high-tensile Aramid fibre core for superior strength and dimensional stability, preformed and compressed under heavy load. It is specifically designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications where reliable sealing performance is critical.

This high-density packing is widely used in fertilizer industry applications involving ammonia, urea, and carbamate services. The Aramid yarn is treated with antifriction fluoropolymer and break-in lubricant, while the PTFE faces are lubricated with inert high-temperature-resistant compounds. This combination ensures low friction, reduced equipment wear, and long operational life.
