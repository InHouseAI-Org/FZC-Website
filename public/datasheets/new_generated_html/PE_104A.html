PE 104A

High-Performance Sealing with the Strength of Aramid and the Softness of PTFE

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

PE 104A

Inmarco Style PE 104A is a high-performance gland packing developed for control valves operating in demanding industrial environments. It features a high-strength aramid yarn core that provides excellent mechanical strength, wear resistance, and durability. The outer PTFE jacket ensures low friction, smooth operation, and outstanding chemical compatibility across a wide range of process media.

Enhanced with fluoropolymer dispersion and corrosion inhibitors, PE 104A delivers long-lasting sealing performance even under severe operating conditions. Its advanced construction promotes efficient heat dissipation, minimizes leakage, and provides reliable sealing performance throughout its service life.
