Ring Type Joint

Inmarco Ring Type Joint (RTJ) Gaskets are precision-machined metallic sealing components engineered for critical high-pressure and high-temperature applications across offshore, onshore, petrochemical, refinery, and subsea industries.

Designed for metal-to-metal sealing reliability, RTJ gaskets provide exceptional sealing integrity under severe pressure, thermal cycling, vibration, and corrosive operating conditions. The self-energizing sealing principle improves gasket performance as internal pressure increases, making RTJ gaskets highly suitable for wellhead systems, pressure vessels, subsea pipelines, and critical flange connections.

Manufactured in accordance with API 6A and ASME B16.20 standards, Inmarco RTJ gaskets are available in Oval, Octagonal, RX, and BX profiles to suit various flange groove configurations and service conditions. The gasket materials are carefully selected to ensure compatibility with flange hardness, process media, temperature conditions, and corrosion resistance requirements, ensuring reliable long-term sealing performance in demanding industrial applications.

Type R – Oval

● Original RTJ gasket design with rounded profile configuration
● Suitable for both rounded-bottom and flat-bottom groove flanges
● Provides reliable sealing performance in standard high-pressure applications
● Fully compliant with ASME B16.20 and API 6A standards

Type R – Octagonal

● Improved sealing profile with direct surface contact on 23° groove faces
● Offers enhanced sealing efficiency compared to oval profiles
● Fully interchangeable with Oval profile in flat-bottom groove applications
● Suitable for high-pressure and critical service environments

Type RX

● Pressure-activated adaptation of the R-Octagonal design
● Outer sealing surfaces create initial contact and self-energizing sealing action
● Internal pressure further increases gasket sealing performance
● Designed for improved sealing reliability under elevated pressures

Type BX

● Designed specifically for ultra-high-pressure applications up to 20,000 psi (1,380 bar)
● Compatible only with dedicated BX groove configurations
● Not interchangeable with R or RX groove designs
● Optional pressure equalization hole available for subsea applications
