IN 123

High-Temperature Ceramic Fibre Packing for Extreme Conditions

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

IN 123

Inmarco IN 123 is a ceramic fibre packing rope manufactured from high-quality ceramic fibre refractory oxides. It is specifically designed to provide exceptional thermal and electrical insulation in static sealing applications operating under extreme temperature conditions.

The packing is non-toxic, non-combustible, and electrically non-conductive, making it a safe and environmentally responsible solution for industrial insulation and sealing requirements. Continuous product development has enhanced insulation efficiency while minimizing ignition loss, ensuring long-term reliability in demanding operating environments.

IN 123 is widely used across power generation, steel manufacturing, aluminium processing, cement plants, furnaces, boilers, and other high-temperature industrial applications where dependable thermal insulation and sealing performance are critical.
