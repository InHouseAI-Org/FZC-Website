TECHNICAL DATASHEET

IN 123I

IN 123Iis a ceramic fibre packing rope made from alumina and silica-based refractory oxides with Inconel wire reinforcement. It delivers excellent thermal and electrical insulation, ideal for static applications in high-temperature environments. The product is engineered to withstand harsh industrial conditions with consistent reliability.It is non-toxic, non-combustible, and environmentally safe, ensuring a secure working atmosphere. Continuous R&D has enhanced its insulation efficiency and minimized loss on ignition. Widely used across industries like power, steel, aluminium, and cement, it supports critical sealing and insulation needs.
