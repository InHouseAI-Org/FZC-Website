IN 123I

Premium Ceramic Fibre Packing Rope with Inconel Wire Reinforcement for Extreme Temperature Applications

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

IN 123I

Inmarco IN 123I is a high-temperature ceramic fibre packing rope manufactured from ceramic fibre reinforced with Inconel wire for enhanced mechanical strength and durability. This premium product combines the exceptional thermal insulation properties of ceramic fibres with the superior mechanical strength of Inconel wire reinforcement.

The combination of ceramic fiber insulation and metallic reinforcement provides exceptional resistance to extreme temperatures while maintaining excellent thermal and electrical insulation properties. The product is specifically engineered for static sealing and insulation applications operating under severe thermal conditions.

IN 123I is non-toxic, non-combustible, chemically stable, and environmentally safe. It is widely used in power generation, steel manufacturing, aluminum processing, cement plants, furnace systems, boilers, and other high-temperature industrial equipment where reliable thermal protection and sealing performance are essential.
