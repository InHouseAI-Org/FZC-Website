LAMINAR SEAL

Inmarco Laminar Seal is a precision-engineered sealing solution specifically developed for demanding valve sealing environments, particularly Double Offset Butterfly Valves and Triple Offset Butterfly Valves.

The seal utilizes a robust Metal–Filler–Metal laminated construction, minimizing internal leak paths while providing exceptional sealing integrity under high-cycle and bidirectional flow conditions. Its pressure-activated design enhances sealing performance as system pressure increases, making it ideal for critical shut-off applications.

The combination of metallic strength and resilient sealing materials ensures excellent resistance to fire, blowout, corrosion, and thermal cycling. This design delivers long service life and reliable performance in severe operating conditions commonly encountered in the Oil & Gas, Power Generation, Nuclear, Marine, and Water Treatment industries.
