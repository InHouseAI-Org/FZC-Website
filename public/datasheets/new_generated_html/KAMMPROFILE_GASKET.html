KAMMPROFILE GASKET

Inmarco Kammprofile Gaskets are high-performance metallic gaskets designed with a precision-machined serrated metal core and soft sealing layers bonded to both sealing surfaces. This unique construction combines the mechanical strength of metal with the superior sealing characteristics of soft facing materials, delivering exceptional sealing reliability across a wide range of pressure and temperature conditions.

The concentric serrations concentrate gasket loading and create highly effective sealing barriers while minimizing flange stress and bolt load requirements. Kammprofile gaskets offer excellent resistance to blowout, thermal cycling, pressure fluctuations, and radial shear forces, making them ideal for critical industrial applications.

The reusable metal core allows refurbishment by replacing only the soft sealing layers, significantly reducing maintenance costs and extending service life.
