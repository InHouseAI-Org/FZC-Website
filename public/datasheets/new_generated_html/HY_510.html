TECHNICAL DATASHEET

Style HY 510EC

HY 510EC is a high-performance hybrid packing designed for demanding industrial and marine applications. It is made from PTFE-based fibers with graphite in the molecular structure and Aramid reinforcement, and features a hollow elastomer core that provides enhanced resiliency. This advanced construction ensures reliable sealing and a long service life, particularly in seawater applications.

The packing offers excellent sealing performance and an optimized mechanical design for marine service conditions. Its specialized structure supports consistent operation under harsh environments, delivering durability, stability, and dependable leak-free performance in critical applications. Tested and qualified as per EN 16752 (Centrifugal pumps test procedure for seal packing), Tightness Class T2.

Key Features & Advantages

● Tested and qualified as per EN 16752 (Centrifugal pumps test procedure for seal packing), Tightness Class T2
● Adequate lubrication for low friction runs ensuring smooth operation
● No embrittlement or abrasion to parent equipment for extended equipment life
● Graphite Inblend controls the thermal expansion of PTFE by around 20%, eliminating chances of overheating and extrusion
● Excellent heat dissipation to ensure cool run and guarantee excellent sealing performance
● Hollow elastomer core ensures excellent resiliency for consistent sealing
● Optimized mechanical design for marine service conditions
