Versatile Hybrid Packing with Exceptional Chemical Resistance

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 501

Inmarco HY 501 is a versatile hybrid packing manufactured from Non-Metallic Expanded Pure Graphite yarn with carbon filament in corners. The packing is impregnated with a proprietary graphite-based dispersion and an inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor, providing exceptional chemical resistance, thermal conductivity, and protection against galvanic corrosion.

Corners are reinforced with carbon filament to enhance mechanical strength and extrusion resistance. The packing is chemically inert, self-lubricating, and non-sticky, ensuring smooth operation without causing shaft, stem, or equipment wear.

HY 501 adapts easily to surface irregularities and stuffing box dimensions, delivering reliable sealing performance in both dynamic and static applications. Its unique construction makes it suitable for use across a wide range of pumps and valves operating under demanding industrial conditions.
