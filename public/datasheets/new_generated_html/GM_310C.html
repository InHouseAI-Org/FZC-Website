GM 310C

High Extrusion Resistance Pressure Seal Ring

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

GM 310C

Inmarco Style GM 310C Graphite Pressure Seal Rings are manufactured from ultra-high-purity graphite ribbon without additives or binders. Designed specifically for pressure seal valves, these rings provide outstanding sealing performance under severe temperature and pressure conditions.

The sealing rings are fitted with SS or Inconel end caps on both the top and bottom surfaces to provide exceptional extrusion resistance. They are available in a variety of configurations, including rectangular, U-shape, and I-shape constructions, allowing customization to suit different valve designs and service conditions.

The combination of high-purity graphite and robust metallic reinforcement ensures excellent thermal stability, low oxidation rates, and long service life in demanding industrial applications.
