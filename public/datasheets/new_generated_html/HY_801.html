HY 801

Versatile Packing with Superior Chemical and Thermal Resistance

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

HY 801

HY 801 is a versatile hybrid packing manufactured from Non-Metallic Expanded Pure Graphite Yarn reinforced with high-structural Carbon Filament. The packing offers exceptional chemical resistance, thermal conductivity, and sealing performance in demanding industrial applications.

Impregnated with proprietary THERMOLUBE™ Graphite-Based Dispersion and an Inorganic Passive Corrosion Inhibitor, HY 801 protects equipment against galvanic corrosion while minimizing oxidation and loss on ignition.

The packing adapts easily to surface irregularities, ensuring smooth operation, excellent dry-run capability, and reliable sealing performance across a wide range of pumps and valves. HY 801 is chemically inert, self-lubricating, and tested for fire-safe and corrosion-resistant applications.
