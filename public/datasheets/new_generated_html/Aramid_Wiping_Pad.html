TECHNICAL DATASHEET

Wiping Pad

INMARCO Wiping Pad is manufactured from tough yet smooth copolymer yarn, lubricated with mineral oil and fluoropolymer suspensoid, and reinforced with high-tensile inorganic filaments. Its dense and heavy-duty construction is produced on specially engineered machinery to ensure dimensional accuracy, consistent wiping performance, and extended service life in demanding wire processing applications.

Designed specifically for wire drawing and galvanizing operations, the pad delivers smooth and uniform wiping action while maintaining excellent durability under continuous operating conditions. Its advanced construction provides reliable wiping performance without compromising wire surface quality, coating consistency, or production efficiency.

The Wiping Pad offers superior wear resistance, excellent chemical compatibility, and reliable operation across a broad temperature range, making it an ideal solution for modern wire manufacturing facilities.
