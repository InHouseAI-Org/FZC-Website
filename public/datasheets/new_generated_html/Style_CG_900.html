TECHNICAL DATASHEET

STYLE CG 900

Style CG 900 is a premium flexible expanded graphite fibre packing manufactured from high-purity graphite fibres with elevated carbon content.

The packing is impregnated with proprietary THERMOLUBE dispersion and reinforced with multiple Inconel wires in each strand, providing exceptional mechanical strength, flexibility, and sealing performance.The packing incorporates an inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor that protects equipment against galvanic corrosion while minimizing loss on ignition.

Its soft yet resilient construction enables easy installation around small-diameter shafts and spindles while maintaining excellent sealing integrity under demanding operating conditions.Style CG 900 is specifically designed for high-temperature and high-pressure applications where superior thermal conductivity, chemical resistance, and fugitive emission control are critical. The packing is tested and certified as per SHELL MESC SPEC 77/312 for fugitive emission performance.

Key Features

●Premium expanded graphite fibre packing with high carbon content.
●Reinforced with multiple Inconel wires in each strand for enhanced mechanical strength.
●Impregnated with proprietary THERMOLUBE dispersion for improved saleability and reduced leakage.
