PA 106

High-Performance Abrasion & Brine Resistant Packing for Extreme Conditions

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

PA 106

Style PA 106 is a high-performance packing manufactured from abrasion-resistant co-polymer fibre yarn and impregnated with PTFE-based Inflon dispersion. It provides exceptional resistance to brine, corrosive chemicals, acidic media, and abrasive environments, making it an ideal sealing solution for demanding industrial applications.

Enhanced with proprietary break-in and run-in lubricants, PA 106 delivers low-friction operation while protecting sleeves and shafts from wear and erosion. Its robust construction maintains dimensional stability under high pressure and temperature conditions, ensuring reliable sealing performance, extended service life, and reduced maintenance requirements.

Also Available in Economical Version
This product is also available in an Economical version as PA 106E, offering cost-effective sealing solutions while maintaining reliable performance standards.
Also Available with Elastomeric Core
This product is also available with an elastomeric core as PA 106EC, providing enhanced resiliency for rugged and resilient operations in demanding applications.
