Performance Benefits

High-Temperature Non-Asbestos Fibre Sheet for Extreme Applications

● NA 442 is a premium compressed non-asbestos carbon-filled gasket sheet manufactured using carbon fibres bonded with NBR. The carbon Fibre reinforcement significantly enhances the material's performance under high-temperature and high-pressure operating conditions.
● Manufactured to BS 7531 Grade Y, NA 442 provides excellent sealing reliability, superior stress relaxation characteristics, and long-term mechanical stability in demanding industrial applications involving steam, hydrocarbons, oils, gases, and aggressive process media.
