OR 151

Duplex Braided Vegetable Fibre Packing with Proprietary PTFE Dispersion

TECHNICAL DATASHEET

OR 151

Inmarco Style OR 151 is a soft vegetable fibre (flax) yarn duplex braided packing impregnated with a special proprietary PTFE-based dispersion incorporating performance-enhancing additives. During manufacturing, each yarn is individually impregnated with the dispersion before braiding, followed by a final surface treatment with PTFE dispersion and special additives to maximize sealing efficiency and service life.

Manufactured from vegetable fibre naturally compatible with water-based applications, OR 151 provides excellent resistance to deterioration in wet environments. The PTFE-based treatment imparts outstanding anti-friction properties, minimizing sleeve and shaft wear while ensuring reliable sealing performance in pumps and marine equipment.
