SHIM GASKET

Inmarco Shim Gaskets are precision-manufactured thin metallic components engineered for critical alignment, spacing, sealing, and tolerance-control applications across industrial equipment and mechanical assemblies.

Manufactured using high-precision die-punching, CNC machining, and laser-cutting technologies, these shim gaskets deliver exceptional dimensional accuracy, flatness, and surface finish required for demanding engineering applications. Designed to provide reliable load distribution, flange alignment correction, gap compensation, and precision spacing, Inmarco Shim Gaskets are widely used in aerospace, oil & gas, petrochemical, marine, automotive, pharmaceutical, and heavy engineering industries.

Available in multiple metallic materials and thickness configurations, these gaskets ensure excellent dimensional stability, corrosion resistance, thermal stability, and long-term operational reliability under varying industrial conditions.
