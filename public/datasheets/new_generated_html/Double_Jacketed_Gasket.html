INMARCO Double Jacketed Gaskets are semi-metallic sealing solutions consisting of a soft filler material completely enclosed within a metallic outer jacket. This construction combines the compressibility and recovery characteristics of non-metallic fillers with the strength, blowout resistance, and durability of metallic materials.

● The metal jacket protects the filler from process media while providing excellent mechanical strength and corrosion resistance. These gaskets are particularly suitable for narrow flange widths, rough or uneven flange faces, and applications involving high temperatures, thermal cycling, and aggressive process conditions.
● Double Jacketed Gaskets are widely used in heat exchangers, boilers, pressure vessels, pumps, valves, steam systems, and process equipment where reliable sealing performance is essential.
