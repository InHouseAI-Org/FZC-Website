ULTRA FE 1003

INMARCO ULTRA FE 1003 is a virtually Zero friction and ultra-low emission Combination Packing Set made from expanded graphite Inconel wire mesh jacketed yarn for end rings and graphite foil ribbon moulded intermediate rings. The packing is impregnated with a high temperature proprietary emulsion to ensure extremely low friction.

The packing set may be impregnated with an inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor to ensure corrosion free performance for prolonged period. This is the world's most innovative and unrivaled low emission packing, API 622 3rd edition qualified, and the first packing globally certified as per API 622 Annex C (HT) 2022 requirement from Yarmouth Research and Technology, USA.

Key Features

● API 622 3rd edition qualified with virtually Zero friction even after 1510 cycles with 10 ppm maximum leakage for 1/4" and 12 ppm for 1/8"
● First packing globally certified as per API 622 Annex C (HT) 2022 requirement from Yarmouth Research and Technology, USA
● Virtually Zero friction even at very high endurance with tested friction factor of 0.1
● Zero corrosion in both ambient and high temperature when tested as per API 622 3rd edition
● Certified for Fire safe compliance as per API 607 8th edition with Zero leakage during and after burning
● Certified as per ISO-15848 with tightness class AH up to CO2 endurance without adjustment
● In-built oxidation inhibitor prevents oxidation loss even at high temperature with very high TQ39 Rating
