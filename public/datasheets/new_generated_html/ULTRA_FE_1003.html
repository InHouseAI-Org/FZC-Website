TECHNICAL DATASHEET

ULTRA FE 1003

Inmarco ULTRA FE 1003 is an advanced ultra-low emission combination packing set engineered for critical valve sealing applications requiring extremely low leakage, virtually zero friction, superior fire safety, and long-term operational reliability under severe industrial service conditions.

The packing set is manufactured using expanded graphite Inconel wire mesh jacketed yarn for end rings combined with graphite foil ribbon molded intermediate rings. The advanced construction delivers exceptional sealing integrity, pressure resistance, thermal stability, and ultra-low fugitive emission performance while maintaining minimal friction under extended cycling conditions.

The packing is impregnated with a proprietary high-temperature emulsion to ensure extremely low friction characteristics and may additionally incorporate an inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor to support corrosion-free long-term operation. ULTRA FE 1003 is globally recognized as one of the most advanced fugitive emission packings, qualified to API 622 3rd Edition standards packing is specifically designed for demanding industrial services including hydrocarbons, steam, aggressive gases, high-temperature fluids, and supercritical CO₂ applications.
