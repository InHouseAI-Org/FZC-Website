PA 104A

PA 104A is a high-performance gland packing developed for control valves in demanding industrial environments. It features an aramid yarn core that offers excellent mechanical strength and wear resistance. The outer PTFE jacket ensures smooth operation and chemical compatibility across various media.

Enhanced with fluoro polymer dispersion and corrosion inhibitors, PA 104A delivers long-lasting sealing performance. Its construction allows effective heat dissipation and minimal leakage. Style PA 104A delivers consistent sealing efficiency and long service life.
