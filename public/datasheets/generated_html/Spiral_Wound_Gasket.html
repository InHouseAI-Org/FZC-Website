Spiral Wound Gasket

INMARCO SPIRAL WOUND GASKET features premium spiral wound construction with alternating layers of V-shaped metal winding and soft filler material. Recognized as the industry standard for high-temperature and high-pressure flange sealing, these gaskets offer excellent resilience and recovery properties. The unique spiral design allows the gasket to recover after compression, maintaining sealing integrity through thermal cycling and pressure fluctuations. Available with optional inner and outer guide rings for precise installation and enhanced performance.
