ULTRA NE 1005

INMARCO ULTRA NE 1005 is an Ultra-low emission, all-braided packing set crafted from expanded graphite yarn jacketed with Inconnel wire mesh. It is impregnated with a proprietary high-temperature emulsion to ensure minimal friction and can be optionally treated with an inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor for long-term, corrosion-free performance.

The unique resilient construction delivers exceptionally low friction, resulting in reduced torque requirements and extended valve life — ultimately lowering energy consumption and low maintenance. This is a true next generation all braided packing engineered for a cleaner, greener, and safer future.
