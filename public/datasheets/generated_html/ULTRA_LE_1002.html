ULTRA LE 1002

INMARCO ULTRA LE 1002 is a premium expanded pure graphite (carbon content 99.5 to 99.9%) fiber yarn braided packing, each yarn of which is reinforced with Multiple Inconel wire, thoroughly incorporated with inorganic passive corrosion inhibitor and special lubricating agents. This Passive Corrosion Inhibitor safeguards parent equipment from Galvanic Corrosion and also reduction of loss on ignition.

Each yarn is jacketed with inconnel wire mesh. The reinforcement and jacketing with inconnel wire meshing each yarn enables the packing to withstand extreme mechanical stress and cyclic loads. This packing is designed for applications requiring 100% leakage free performance and fire hazard protection.

Key Features & Approvals

● Passed API 607 and ISO 10497 Fire Safe Tests by Yarmouth Lab, USA
● Passed fugitive emission Test as per SHELL MESC SPE 77/312 to tightness Class B
● Approved as per ISO 15848 - Part I for fugitive emission from FCRI, Palakkad
● Passed API 622 2nd edition from Yarmouth Lab, USA
● Tested for anti VOC compliance
● Special lubricating agents reduces stem friction and corrosion inhibitor prevents pitting due to galvanic corrosion
● Very high sealing efficiency even on oscillating valve stems without much wear and tear
