TECHNICAL DATASHEET

IN 140

IN 140 Fiberglass braided rope is made from premium texturized electrical and chemical resistant special Fiberglass in cover on cover braid. Style 140 is of high tenacity and is meant for static sealing in dry condition.

Can also be used in dynamic condition while treated with special dispersion based on graphite, Vermiculite or PTFE. This also available with SS wire/Inconnel wire reinforced to resist higher pressure and static load. Available in multiple variants for different applications and temperature requirements.

Key Features

● Premium texturized electrical and chemical resistant special fiberglass
● Cover on cover braid construction for high tenacity
● Available with wire reinforcement (SS/Inconnel) for higher pressure resistance
● Can be treated with graphite, Vermiculite or PTFE for dynamic applications
● Custom sizes and lengths available on request
● Available in roll form, square or rectangular cross sections

Applications

● Furnace doors
● Fill glass flange and kiln cover
● Chemical glass flanges in exhaust and chimneys
● Equipment handling highly corrosive, alcohol and solvents
● Not suitable for HFHCL and hot phosphoric acid
