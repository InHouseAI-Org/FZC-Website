ULTRA LT 1004

INMARCO ULTRA LT 1004 is an ultra-low friction low emission PTFE combination packing set made from PTFE filament braided intermediate rings with PTFE sintered end rings. The packing is impregnated with a high temperature proprietary emulsion to ensure extremely low friction even at sub-zero temperature.

This is the world's most innovative and unrivaled low emission packing for low temperature applications, tested and certified as per ISO 15848 for temperature range -46°C to 200°C. The engineered profile of end rings helps in equivalent load transfer for best sealing performance, while virgin PTFE ensures high chemical compatibility.

Key Features & Certifications

● Certified as per ISO15848-CO3-AH-SSA0-t for temperature range -46°C to 200°C
● Certified as per TA-Luft(2021)-CO3-LB-SSA0 for room temperature to 200°C
● Certified as per ISO15848-CC3-AM-SSA1-t for room temperature to 200°C
● Exceptionally low friction to ensure consistent low emission performance even at sub-zero temperatures
● In-built solid lubricant with extremely low coefficient of friction and high load bearing capacity
● Engineered profile of end rings helps in equivalent load transfer for best sealing performance
● Virgin PTFE ensures high chemical compatibility across a wide range of media

Applications

● Acids & alkalis of any concentration
● Solvents
● Organic/inorganic chemicals
● Hydrogen services
● Food & pharma applications
● Low temperature processes and cryogenic applications
