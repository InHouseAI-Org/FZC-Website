HY 105

HY 105 is a high-performance gland packing made from a dense braid of Aramid Yarn corners and PTFE Yarn faces, reinforced with a resilient Aramid fiber core. The materials are treated with advanced lubricants to reduce friction and enhance thermal resistance, ensuring smooth operation and extended service life.

This packing is designed for demanding industrial environments, offering excellent chemical resistance, high pressure tolerance, and dimensional stability. It is ideal for use in pumps, valves, mixers, and reactors across chemical, fertilizer, and process industries.

Also Available in Economical version
This product is also available in an Economical version as HY 105E, offering cost-effective sealing solutions while maintaining reliable performance standards.

Key Features

● Unique combination of aramid yarn corners and PTFE Yarn faces, designed for enhanced sealing performance.
● Reinforced with a high-tensile Aramid fiber core, offering exceptional mechanical strength and dimensional stability.
● PTFE faces treated with inert, high-temperature lubricants to ensure smooth, low-friction operation.
● Aramid corners lubricated with antifrictional fluoropolymer dispersion, preventing corner failures in reciprocating equipment.
● Delivers reliable sealing in chemically aggressive and mechanically demanding environments.

Applications

● Excellent compatibility with a wide range of industrial media including slurries, pigments, and synthetic compounds.
● Designed for consistent performance in centrifugal, reciprocating, and plunger pump systems.
● Ideal for chemical processing, fertilizer production, and pharmaceutical manufacturing.
● Suitable for valves, mixers, agitators, and reactors in demanding process industries.
