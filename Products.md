# Inmarco FZC - Products Catalog

## Product Categories Overview

Inmarco FZC specializes in industrial fluid sealing solutions with a comprehensive portfolio across 6 major product categories. All products are designed for extreme conditions and certified to international standards.

---

## 1. Compression Packing

### Overview
High-performance compression packings engineered for rotating and reciprocating equipment across various industrial applications.

### Product Lines

#### 1.1 Reciprocating Pumps
- **Applications:** Positive displacement pumps, plunger pumps, piston pumps
- **Key Features:**
  - High pressure resistance
  - Low friction coefficient
  - Extended service life
  - Chemical compatibility

#### 1.2 Centrifugal Pumps
- **Applications:** Process pumps, water pumps, chemical transfer pumps
- **Key Features:**
  - Low emission design
  - API 682 compliant options
  - Temperature resistant up to 650°C
  - Pressure ratings up to 350 bar

#### 1.3 Reactors
- **Applications:** Chemical reactors, agitators, mixers
- **Key Features:**
  - Chemical resistance (acids, alkalis, solvents)
  - Tight sealing under agitation
  - FDA compliant grades available
  - Low particulate generation

#### 1.4 Static Sealing
- **Applications:** Manways, vessel covers, pipe joints
- **Key Features:**
  - High compressibility
  - Conformability to irregular surfaces
  - Fire-safe grades
  - Wide temperature range

#### 1.5 Bulk Sealing Compounds
- **Applications:** On-site packing fabrication, emergency repairs
- **Key Features:**
  - Field moldable
  - Quick installation
  - Multiple material grades
  - Long shelf life

### Materials Available
- Graphite
- PTFE
- Aramid fiber
- Carbon fiber
- Hybrid combinations

### Standards & Certifications
- API 622 (Low Emission)
- EN 16752
- ISO 15848-1
- TA-Luft compliant

## 3. Industrial Gaskets

### Overview
Comprehensive range of gasket solutions from non-metallic to metallic designs, engineered for critical flanged connections.

### Product Lines

#### 3.1 Specialty Gasket Insulation Kits (GIKs)
- **Applications:** Insulated flanges, corrosion prevention
- **Key Features:**
  - Electrical isolation
  - NACE compliant materials
  - Full face or ring type
  - Custom dimensions

#### 3.2 Metallic Gaskets
- **Types:**
  - Ring Joint Gaskets (RTJ)
  - Metal Jacketed Gaskets
  - Corrugated Metal Gaskets
  - Solid Metal Gaskets
- **Materials:** Stainless Steel 304/316, Inconel, Monel, Hastelloy
- **Applications:** High pressure, high temperature services
- **Standards:** ASME B16.20, API 6A

#### 3.3 Semi-Metallic Gaskets
- **Types:**
  - Spiral Wound Gaskets (with inner/outer rings)
  - Kammprofile Gaskets
  - Double Jacketed Gaskets
- **Materials:**
  - Core: Graphite, PTFE, Mica
  - Winding: SS 304/316, Inconel
- **Applications:** Pressure vessels, heat exchangers, turbines
- **Standards:** ASME B16.20

#### 3.4 Non-Metallic Gaskets
- **Types:**
  - Compressed Fiber Sheets
  - PTFE Gaskets (virgin, filled, expanded)
  - Rubber Gaskets (NBR, EPDM, FKM, Silicone)
  - Graphite Sheet Gaskets
- **Notable Product:** **NA-333** - Eco-friendly, non-asbestos gasket material
- **Applications:** General industrial, chemical, food grade
- **Certifications:** FDA, WRAS, NSF compliant grades

### Services
- CNC gasket cutting service (custom shapes and sizes)
- Gasket failure analysis
- Material selection support

## 6. Expansion Joints

### Overview
Flexible connectors designed to absorb thermal expansion, vibration, and misalignment in piping systems.

### Product Lines

#### 6.1 Rubber Expansion Joints
- **Types:**
  - Single sphere
  - Double sphere
  - Reducing/concentric
  - Wide arch
- **Applications:** HVAC, water treatment, pump connections
- **Features:**
  - Absorb axial, lateral, angular movements
  - Vibration isolation
  - Noise reduction
- **Materials:** EPDM, NBR, Neoprene, Natural rubber
- **Pressure Rating:** Up to 16 bar
- **Size Range:** DN25 to DN3000

#### 6.2 Metal Expansion Joints
- **Types:**
  - Single bellows
  - Universal (double bellows)
  - Pressure balanced
  - Hinged/gimbal
- **Applications:** High temperature piping, steam lines, exhaust systems
- **Materials:** Stainless Steel 304/316, Inconel
- **Temperature Range:** Up to 850°C
- **Pressure Rating:** Up to 40 bar

#### 6.3 Fabric Expansion Joints
- **Types:**
  - Rectangular
  - Round
  - Custom shapes
- **Applications:** Flue gas ducts, power plant ducting, cement plants
- **Materials:** Fiberglass fabric, silicone-coated fabric
- **Temperature Range:** Up to 1000°C
- **Features:**
  - Lightweight
  - Large movement capability
  - Corrosion resistant
