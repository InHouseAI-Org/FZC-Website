# Video Instructions for Company Overview Section

## 📹 Video Requirements

The Company Overview section is now configured to display a **facility/team video** showing your manufacturing environment and workers. This helps build trust and showcase your professional operations.

### Video Specifications

- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 or similar
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 (Full HD) or higher
- **Duration:** 10-30 seconds (will loop)
- **File Size:** Keep under 10MB for fast loading
- **Orientation:** Landscape (horizontal)
- **Audio:** Not needed (video will be muted)

## 🎬 How to Find the Perfect Video

### Option 1: Pexels (Recommended - Completely Free)

1. Visit: **https://www.pexels.com/search/videos/industrial/**

2. **Search Terms to Try:**
   - "factory workers"
   - "manufacturing facility"
   - "industrial workers"
   - "precision manufacturing"
   - "industrial production"
   - "assembly line"
   - "metalworking factory"
   - "industrial engineer"

3. **What to Look For:**
   - ✅ Clean, modern manufacturing environment
   - ✅ Workers in professional attire (safety gear, uniforms)
   - ✅ Well-lit facility
   - ✅ Close-up or medium shots of people working
   - ✅ Smooth camera movements (panning, slow-motion)
   - ✅ Professional industrial equipment in background
   - ❌ Avoid dark or messy environments
   - ❌ Avoid extreme close-ups that lose context
   - ❌ Avoid shaky handheld footage

4. **Download:**
   - Click on the video you like
   - Select "Free Download"
   - Choose "HD" or "Full HD" quality
   - No attribution required!

### Option 2: Pixabay

1. Visit: **https://pixabay.com/videos/search/industrial/**
2. Similar search process as Pexels
3. Also completely free, no attribution needed

### Option 3: Coverr.co

1. Visit: **https://coverr.co/**
2. Browse "Business" or "Technology" categories

### Step 1: Download Video

2. The file will likely be named something like `pexels-video-12345.mp4`

### Step 2: Place in Project
1. Rename the file to: **`facility-video.mp4`**
2. Place it in the **public folder** of your project:

## 🎨 Video Optimization Tips

### Before Uploading:
If your video file is too large (over 10MB), compress it:

**Option A: HandBrake (Free Desktop App)**
1. Download: https://handbrake.fr/
2. Open your video
3. Use "Web" preset
4. Export

**Option B: Online Tools**
- cloudconvert.com
- freeconvert.com
- clideo.com

**Recommended Settings:**
- Resolution: 1920x1080
- Bitrate: 2-4 Mbps
- Frame Rate: 30fps
- Format: MP4 (H.264)

## 📝 Alternative: Use Your Own Video

If you have footage of your actual facility:

### DIY Video Recording Tips:
1. **Use a smartphone** in landscape mode
2. **Stabilize** - use a tripod or steady surface
3. **Good lighting** - natural daylight or well-lit facility
4. **10-20 second clips** are perfect
5. **Slow, smooth movements** - pan across facility slowly
6. Show:
   - Workers doing precision work
   - Clean, organized workspace
   - Modern equipment
   - Quality control processes
   - Team collaboration

### What to Capture:
- ✅ Worker examining gaskets/seals
- ✅ Manufacturing process in action
- ✅ Quality inspection
- ✅ Packaging/final products
- ✅ Clean facility overview
